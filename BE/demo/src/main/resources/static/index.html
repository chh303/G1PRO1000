Efficient Coding

Write clean, readable, and efficient code to reduce unnecessary resource usage. Simple and well-structured solutions make systems easier to maintain and develop further, while also requiring less processing power.

Optimize loops, avoid redundant operations, and choose algorithms and data structures that fit the task. Smart use of caching and reduced I/O usage can contribute to lower energy consumption and more sustainable software development.

Sustainable Servers

Choose hosting services and data centers powered by renewable energy sources. Many providers now offer greener alternatives that significantly reduce the carbon footprint.

Services like AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure have their own sustainability initiatives and offer options to select climate-friendly regions. Making conscious infrastructure choices can have a major impact on overall energy consumption.

Energy-Efficient Backend

Reduce server load by using caching and minimizing unnecessary requests. This leads to faster response times and lower energy consumption.

Optimize your database with indexing and efficient queries, and avoid heavy processing on the server side. Smart choices in backend architecture support both performance and sustainability.
